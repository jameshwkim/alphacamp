About Us

Company Overview

Knight Chartering (Singapore) Pte Ltd is a subsidiary of Knight Chartering DA, Norway.

Knight Chartering is a Corporate and Commercial management company for the vessels owned and operated by Knight Shipping.

Our dynamic and experienced team are known for proactive approach toward clients’ needs and requirements.

We are committed to providing highest levels of customer satisfaction to our customers with particular emphasis on the safety of the ship, the cargo, the crew and the environment.

The Knight Shipping Group consists of the following companies; Knight Shipping AS (head owner), Knight Chartering DA, Knight Chartering (Singapore), GSH 1 Chem-Prod Carrier II AS, GSH1 Chem-Prod Carrier III AS, Hawk Explore AS, Chem Glory AS, PCTC Holding AS.

Mission and Vision

We believe in leadership through quality operations, and strive to provide a leading ship chartering and ship management service in the global markets.

Mission

We aim to achieve high quality, consistent reliability and efficient transportation to all users of maritime services.

Vision

Knight Chartering will generate value for our business partners and establish the company as a trusted and consistently performing service provider in the shipping industry.
